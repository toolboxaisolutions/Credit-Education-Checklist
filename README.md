# Credit Comeback — The Guided Credit Repair System

> **Tagline:** *"Your credit didn't break overnight. Fix it step by step — and watch it come back."*

## What This Is

A self-service SaaS product ($22/mo) that guides people with bad credit through an interactive, gamified repair process. No vague advice. No "figure it out yourself." Every step is explicit, sequential, and verifiable.

## Core Philosophy

- **Zero ambiguity** — every task tells the user EXACTLY what to do
- **Visual progress** — node-based journey map on the left, current task on the right
- **Gate logic** — can't advance past critical dependencies until prerequisites are done; some steps parallelize
- **Automated everything** — self-serve signup, autopay, cancellation, progress tracking
- **Credit report integration** — pull reports, show score trajectory over time (potential upsell)
- **Personalized** — lifestyle-based credit card recommendations, spending habit questions
